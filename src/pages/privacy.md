# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 29, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 29, 2026

## Overview

Serverly ("the App") is a native iOS application that runs a local SQL database server on your device. Your privacy is a core design principle of this app.

**The short version:** Serverly does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data or database contents. Everything stays on your device.

---

## Information We Collect

**We collect no personal information.**

Serverly does not:
- Collect your name, email address, or any account information
- Require you to create an account
- Track your usage or behavior within the app
- Collect analytics or crash reports from your device
- Access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or location
- Transmit any data to external servers

---

## Your Database Data

All data you store in Serverly — including database files, tables, queries, and schemas — is stored exclusively in your iOS app sandbox (`Documents/mysql-data/`). This data:

- Never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself
- Is not accessible to Serverly (the company or developer)
- Is not backed up to any server we control
- May be backed up to iCloud if you have iCloud backups enabled for your device (this is controlled entirely by Apple and your iOS settings)

---

## Network Usage

Serverly binds a server to your **local network only** while the app is active in the foreground. This server:

- Is accessible only to devices on the same local Wi-Fi or network
- Does not connect to the internet
- Stops serving when the app is backgrounded or closed
- Does not phone home to any Serverly infrastructure

---

## Third-Party Services

Serverly does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

---

## Children's Privacy

Serverly is a developer tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and post the new policy here. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
